Grilled Cheese

Easy, Cheap, Delicious, Classic

Ingredients

  • Bread, sliced, any kind
  • Cheese, any variety that melts properly
  • Butter (or butter-like product) obviously

Steps

  1. Heat frying pan over medium heat
  2. Add butter to hot pan
  3. Assemble sandwich as desired
  4. Apply butter to one face of the sandwich
  5. Add sandwich to pan, buttered side down
  6. Let cook for 2 or 3 minutes
  7. While first side cooks, apply butter to unbuttered face
  8. Flip sandwich so newly buttered face is face-down in the pan
  9. Cook for 2-3 minutes
  10. Remove from heat, allow to cool and enjoy
