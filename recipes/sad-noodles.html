Sad Noodles

This is the bottom of the barrel of poverty foods you can eat, and now you can learn how to make it too!

Ingredients:

  • The cheapest ramen you can find
  • The included seasoning packet
  • The cleanest water you can find

Steps:

  1. Get out your only sauce pan
  2. Fill that bitch up with some clean water, if possible
  3. Boil that water to make it even cleaner
  4. Once boiling, quickly unwrap the ramen noodles but be sure to spill crumbs everywhere
  5. Throw that sad square of carbs into the boiling water
  6. Wait, you god damn animal, for two minutes
  7. Struggle to open the seasoning packet but eventually pour it all in the pan and stir for an additional minute
  8. Eat the sad noodles directly out of the pan, then drink the broth to sustain your weak body
