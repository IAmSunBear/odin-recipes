Sad Spaghet

Got like $4? then you too can eat!

Ingredients

  • Box of spaghetti
  • Jar of pasta sauce or ketchup if you're already drunk
  • Water, clean if possible

Steps

  1. Boil water, especially if you couldn't get any that was clean
  2. Once boiling, add the pasta and stir occasionally for 9 minutes or 10 minutes if your teeth are weak
  3. While the pasta cooks, add pasta sauce to a small sauce pan and cook over low heat, stirring frequently to avoid being splashed by scalding hot sauce, unless you're into that, no judgement
  4. Drain pasta and add sauce
  5. Eat directly from the pan like a rat
